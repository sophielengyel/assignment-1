The Life and Career of Kendrick Lamar

Kendrick Lamar is an American rapper, songwriter, and record producer born in 1987 in Compton, California. Known for his intricate lyricism and socially conscious themes, Lamar has become one of the most influential voices in hip-hop.

More Information

Early Life and Career

Kendrick Lamar Duckworth grew up in Compton, a city with a rich hip-hop history. Inspired by artists like Tupac Shakur and Dr. Dre, Lamar began rapping at a young age.

Kendrick's Rise to Fame

Following Section.80, Lamar released good kid, m.A.A.d city (2012), a concept album that detailed his upbringing in Compton.
